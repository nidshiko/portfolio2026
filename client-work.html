RKD Group — Connaxis

Massachusetts General Hospital

Direct mail · Production Design · RKD Group

Massachusetts General Hospital is one of the most established healthcare brands in the United States. The work shown here spans multiple direct mail campaigns produced at Connaxis for RKD Group, including annual giving, major gifts, and acquisition packages.

My role operates at the intersection of design and production, collaborating closely with art directors, creative directors, copywriters, and account teams to develop campaign materials and carry them through to print. The focus is on translating creative into production-ready systems — managing art edits, ensuring spec compliance, and overseeing prepress and final delivery. The work demands precision at scale, where consistency and accuracy are critical across high-volume outputs.

Newsletters

Print · Newsletter design · RKD Group

Newsletter work follows the same production model developed for RKD Group, with slightly more room for creative input — particularly in layout, hierarchy, and visual balance — while still operating within established brand frameworks.

Hogarth

DirecTV

Digital production

DirecTV campaign materials produced at Hogarth — digital assets including email templates, web banners, social media content and on-screen graphics. Each format came with its own technical constraints, requiring the visual system to be adapted across multiple sizes, resolutions and contexts. The goal was to maintain consistency while translating the same campaign across a varied digital ecosystem. The final set shows graphics for Channel 100 — DirecTV's exclusive subscriber channel, used to deliver promotional and entertainment content directly to users.
