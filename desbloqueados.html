01 Packaging Illustration Identity

Desbloqueados

Complete visual identity and packaging design for a children's board game. Character design, color system, and print-ready artwork developed at FADU-UBA.

Desbloqueados is a board game designed for children aged 5 and up. The brief called for a complete visual system — packaging, game pieces, and instructional materials — that felt energetic and playful without being generic.

The identity is built on geometric character faces that appear across all surfaces of the box, creating a sense of movement and discovery as you rotate the packaging. Each face uses a distinct color combination drawn from a cohesive palette of primary and secondary colors.

The typographic treatment uses a bold rounded wordmark that references pixel art and arcade culture, grounding the design in a world kids recognize.
