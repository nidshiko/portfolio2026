About

Design with a dark side

I'm a Senior Production Designer and illustrator based in Buenos Aires. I graduated from FADU-UBA and work at the intersection of editorial design, illustration, and production.

My personal work lives between the cute and the uncanny — analog materials, mythological sensibility, objects that exist in the physical world. I make books, systems, zines, and worlds.

I believe the most interesting design happens at the edges — between kawaii and monstrous, between production and authorship, between the brief and the thing you actually wanted to make.
