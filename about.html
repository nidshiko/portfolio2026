About

I'm a Graphic Designer and illustrator based in Buenos Aires. I graduated from FADU-UBA and have spent the past years working at a design agency — building systems, solving complex problems at scale, and making sure every detail holds up.

I approach design as a form of communication. Whether it's a client campaign or a self-published zine about pole sport, the goal is the same: clarity, precision, and intent.

My personal work — zines, illustrated books, fanzines — exists because I need it to. Not to fill a portfolio, but to explore, to think, and to expand. It's where I test ideas, take risks, and let things be less controlled. Somehow, it all connects.
